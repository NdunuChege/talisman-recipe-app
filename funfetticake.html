Home Funfetti cake

Ingredients

  • Flour (2 ½ cups): All-purpose flour provides structure.
  • Sugar (1 ½ cups): Granulated sugar for sweetness.
  • Butter (½ cup, softened): Adds richness and flavor.
  • Eggs (3 large): Bind the batter and provide structure
  • Milk (1 cup): Adds moisture.
  • Vanilla Extract (2 teaspoons): Classic flavor
  • Baking Powder (2 teaspoons): For leavening.
  • Salt (½ teaspoon): Enhances the flavor
  • Rainbow Sprinkles (½ cup): Essential for the funfetti look.

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two round cake pans.
  2. Whisk together dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, and salt) in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk, mixing until smooth.
  6. Fold in sprinkles gently to avoid color bleeding
  7. Divide batter evenly between pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
  8. Cool completely before frosting with vanilla buttercream
