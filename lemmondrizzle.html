Home lemondrizzle cake

Ingredients

  • Flour (1 ½ cups): Self-raising flour is commonly used.
  • Sugar (1 cup): Granulated sugar for sweetness
  • Butter (¾ cup, softened): Adds richness and moisture
  • Eggs (3 large): Bind the ingredients and provide structure
  • Lemon Zest (2 lemons): Provides fresh citrus flavor
  • Milk (3 tablespoons): Adds moisture
  • Baking Powder (1 teaspoon): Helps the cake rise

Drizzle Ingredients

  • Lemon Juice (2 lemons): For the tart flavor
  • Powdered Sugar (1 cup): To sweeten the drizzle

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan
  2. Beat butter and sugar until creamy, then add eggs one at a time.
  3. Stir in lemon zest and milk
  4. Fold in flour and baking powder until just combined
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 40-45 minutes
  6. Combine lemon juice and powdered sugar for the drizzle
  7. While the cake is warm, poke holes in the top and pour the drizzle over it
  8. Cool completely before serving
