Home Bundt cake

Ingredients

  • Flour (2 ½ cups): Provides structure; all-purpose flour is commonly used.
  • Sugar (2 cups): Granulated sugar adds sweetness and helps with the cake's texture
  • Butter (1 cup, softened): Unsalted butter offers richness and moisture
  • Eggs (4 large): Bind the ingredients and add structure and flavor.
  • Milk or Buttermilk (1 cup): Adds moisture; buttermilk enhances tenderness and provides a subtle tang.
  • Baking Powder (2 teaspoons): Acts as a leavening agent to help the cake rise.
  • Salt (½ teaspoon): Balances sweetness and enhances overall flavor.
  • Vanilla Extract (2 teaspoons): Adds a classic flavor; other extracts like almond or citrus can be used.
  • Optional Add-ins: Lemon/orange zest, spices (e.g., cinnamon, nutmeg), chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits for variety.

Preparation

  1. Grease and flour a Bundt pan thoroughly to prevent sticking.
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, and salt) in a separate bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy for proper aeration.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  5. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in vanilla extract or other flavorings, ensuring the batter is smooth and well-mixed.
  7. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared Bundt pan and smooth the top.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 50-60 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  9. Cool the cake in the pan for 15-20 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack.
  10. Optionally, prepare a glaze with powdered sugar and milk or cream, then drizzle it over the cooled cake before serving.
