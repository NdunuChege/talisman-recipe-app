Home Hummingbird cake

Ingredients

  • Flour (3 cups): All-purpose flour as the base.
  • Sugar (2 cups): Granulated sugar for sweetness
  • Pineapple (1 cup, crushed): Adds moisture and tropical flavor
  • Mashed Bananas (1 cup): Provides sweetness and tenderness
  • Vegetable Oil (1 cup): Keeps the cake moist
  • Eggs (3 large): Binds ingredients
  • Baking Soda (1 teaspoon): Leavening agent
  • Salt (½ teaspoon): Balances the sweetness
  • Cinnamon (1 teaspoon): Adds warmth and spice
  • Vanilla Extract (1 teaspoon): Enhances flavor
  • Chopped Pecans (1 cup): Adds crunch and texture.

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two round cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt) in a bowl
  3. In another bowl, combine oil, eggs, mashed bananas, pineapple, and vanilla
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture
  5. Fold in chopped pecans.
  6. Divide butter evenly between pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
  7. Cool completely and frost with cream cheese frosting
