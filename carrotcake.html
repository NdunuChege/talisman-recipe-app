Home Carrot cake

Ingredients

  • Flour (2 ½ cups): Provides structure; use all-purpose flour.
  • Sugar (1 ½ cups): Granulated sugar or a mix of granulated and brown sugar adds sweetness and moisture.
  • Carrots (2 cups, grated): Adds moisture, sweetness, and flavor.
  • Vegetable Oil (1 cup): Keeps the cake moist and tender.
  • Eggs (4 large): Bind ingredients and add structure
  • Baking Powder (2 teaspoons) & Baking Soda (1 teaspoon): Leavening agents for a light texture.
  • Salt (½ teaspoon): Balances the sweetness.
  • Cinnamon (2 teaspoons) & Nutmeg (½ teaspoon): Provide warm spice flavors.
  • Vanilla Extract (2 teaspoons): Enhances overall flavor.
  • Optional Add-ins: Chopped nuts, raisins, or shredded coconut for texture and variety

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two round cake pans or one Bundt pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices, and salt) in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla until combined
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture
  5. Fold in grated carrots and any optional add-ins
  6. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 35-40 minutes (round pans) or 50-60 minutes (Bundt pan)
  7. Cool completely before frosting with cream cheese frosting
